Our Mission

Demographic

Our platform supports people dealing with mental health struggles, academic difficulties, discrimination, fear of college preparation, and other personal hurdles. We focus on young adolescents and teenagers who feel isolated or helpless. We also welcome adults facing unique situations, such as significant life changes.

✖

The Problem

Many people struggle in everyday life but hesitate to seek help due to societal pressure, discrimination, or unsupportive environments. Often, they lack access to someone experienced in their specific situation, making it hard to find relatable support.

Our Solution

We offer an anonymous platform where users can share experiences without fear of judgment. Our strict safety measures ensure a secure environment. In addition to peer support, we provide access to professional help, with an optional paid feature for specialized assistance. At TEAA By Teens, we provide a safe space for individuals to find understanding, support, and professional help, empowering them to navigate their unique challenges.
